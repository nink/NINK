What is Neural Intelligence?

Neural Intelligence is the next layer beyond AI. Artificial intelligence can generate answers — Neural Intelligence validates, improves, and learns through a network of people, data, and systems.

Artificial Intelligence

Predicts. Generates. Suggests.

Neural Intelligence

Validates. Connects. Learns. Builds trust.

Neural Intelligence combines human knowledge, artificial intelligence, networked validation, real-world data, community feedback, and continuous learning — so decisions get better over time, not just faster.

First proof point: Retail

NINK DealCheck

Our first Neural Intelligence product for grocery retail.

DealCheck helps shoppers know if a price is really a deal by combining flyer data, product matching, barcode scans, regular prices, historical prices, AI, and human validation.
