Why it is different

Most apps show you a flyer price. DealCheck asks a harder question: is this actually a good deal?

  • Compares flyer promos to regular shelf prices, not just the strikethrough on the flyer.
  • Matches products across multiple retailers — same item, different stores.
  • Uses barcode scans for ground-truth product identity.
  • Combines AI extraction with human validation before trusting catalog links.
  • Builds on historical pricing and weekly flyer cycles, not one-off screenshots.

How Neural Intelligence improves grocery shopping

DealCheck is NINK's first Neural Intelligence product. It connects flyer data, retailer catalogs, barcode identity, and human review into one decision layer — so shoppers get answers they can trust, not just predictions.
